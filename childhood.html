Early Years

I was born and raised in a vibrant community where laughter, curiosity, and adventure filled my days. My childhood was shaped by the warmth of family, the thrill of discovery, and the lessons learned from both play and responsibility.

Family & Traditions

Growing up, family gatherings were the heart of our home. We celebrated holidays with music, storytelling, and delicious food. My grandparents played a huge role in teaching me values like kindness, perseverance, and respect.

Formative Moments

One of my most memorable experiences was building my first toy car from scrap materials. It sparked my love for creating and problem-solving. School trips, neighborhood games, and helping out at home all contributed to the person I am today.
